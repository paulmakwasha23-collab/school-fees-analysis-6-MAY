Harare Birth Registry Analysis — May 2026¶

1. Executive Summary¶

This report analyzes 3,000 birth registry records in Harare to identify gaps in civil registration. The current registration rate sits at 58.2%, indicating a significant portion of the population remains unregistered.

2. Data Cleaning & Ingestion¶

  • Dataset Size: 3,000 rows, 20 columns.
  • Cleaning: Handled missing values in Parent Education and Religious Affiliation by categorizing as "Unknown." Verified zero duplicate records.

# 1. Group by Ward and calculate the registration rate and average distance
ward_stats = df.groupby('Ward_or_Suburb').agg(
    Registration_Rate=('Registration_Status', 'mean'),
    Avg_Distance=('Distance_To_Registry_Office', 'mean')
).reset_index()

# 2. Sort the results to see the areas with the LOWEST registration rates first
worst_performing_wards = ward_stats.sort_values(by='Registration_Rate').head()

# 3. Print the results
print("Wards with the Lowest Registration Rates:")
print(worst_performing_wards)
